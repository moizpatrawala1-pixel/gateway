The Gateway Legacy

1975

Since Day One

Crafting Tins for
Generations

Founded by Ismail Sulemanji Patrawala in 1975 in a modest factory in Lower Parel, Gateway Metal Industries was built on the core belief that packaging is a reflection of trust, durability, and care.

As a leading metal tin packaging supplier, we expanded and shifted operations to a state-of-the-art facility in Taloja in 2009. Today, we proudly serve hundreds of customers worldwide — from FMCG and pharmaceuticals to food colours, saffron, spices, and industrial adhesives.
